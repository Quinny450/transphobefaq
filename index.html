Transphobe FAQ

What is this?

This website is a list of talking points typically used by transphobes (people who dislike or fear transgender individuals), along with their respective rebuttals, accompanied by relevant credible sources.

Transgender people go through a bit of strife as a result of statements typically used in political arguments. There's lots of chaos around those discussions and it's hard to tell for sure what's what. Whether you're a transphobe, transgender, or simply a concerned parent looking to learn more, I hope you'll find what you're looking for here.
